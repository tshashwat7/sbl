Welcome to StemBridge Labs

To Help Educational institutions in promotion of quality and growth


StemBridge Labs is technology driven, STEM-based hands-on learning services Provider Company equipping the ecosystem of K-12 Teaching and learning practices by focusing on raising the standard of quality school education by enhancing knowledge based education system rather than conventional information based existing scenario.

We are determined to Bridge gap between theoretical approach in schools today and knowledge based schools education system of tomorrow.

Our solutions are the State of Art, Easy to customize and tailor made solutions for diversified need of students. We provide our offerings as an umbrella of educational services for each stakeholders of our target business segment i.e. School education which includes students, teachers, school administration and parents by providing them.

Our Offerings


We intend to enable critical thinking through our offerings

Project Based Learning

Our workshops promote Project Based Learning (PBL) to foster imagination, curiosity and creativity in students. We organise workshops in schools where students get indulged in a different kind of learning through experimentation.

STEM Studio

Our STEM Laboratories or Stem Studio is a learning hub where major emphasis is put on Robotics and 3D Printing. Students can enrol in this 10-month program to practice experimentation and apply their STEM skills in different kinds of projects.

Atal Tinkering Lab

Atal Tinkering Laboratories are being established in schools across India under Atal Innovation Mission – a Government of India initiative promoting innovation and entrepreneurship. We are official vendors of NITI Ayog to organise laboratories across schools.

Winter/Summer Camp

We organise Winter Camps which are more like regular workshops, but lasts for a longer duration. These are week long programs devised to foster innovativeness among students. Like winter camps, we also organise summer camps

Internet of Things

Internet of Things is one of the most demanded technologies in today’s world. Workshops on Internet of Things are usually organized in engineering colleges to fetch better understanding of this emergent technology in students

School in Analytics(SIA)

This is a software solution that StemBridgeLabs offers to streamline their operations and ensure better management. Schools can record and maintain all information related to students, attendance, transportation, teachers and others.
