Roadmap For Collaboration

Atal Tinkering Lab

StemBridge Labs is the official partner in this initiative and helps organize Labs across schools. The workspaces are dynamic where young minds can generate creative ideas through hands-on projects, and get an edge in innovation. All tools and equipment available at these labs are used to understand the concepts of STEM, and contain do it yourself kits. Students also get other desirable amenities such as meeting rooms and video conferencing facilities.

The labs feature state-of-the-art equipment such as Robotics & Electronics Development Tools, 3D Printers, IoT, and Sensors etc. The equipment and activities cultivate critical thinking in students and allow them to explore beyond their curriculum or textbook learning. This is more futuristic by allowing students to explore the skills of tomorrow such as design and computation thinking, artificial intelligence and adaptive learning. This is possible through STEM-based learning, which combines four disciplines – Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics – into one unit.
