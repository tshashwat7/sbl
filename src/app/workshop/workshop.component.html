Project Based Learning Workshops

A student-centered pedagogy, Project-based learning (PBL) is considered an alternative to the teacher led classroom model. In fact, this will integrate theory and practice. The benefits are plenty including improved writing skill, understanding concepts, people and processes in an organization.

StemBridge Labs is proud to offer the in-house school developed product “Make It Yourself” which has evolved in learning over project-based programs. it demands students engagement in problem-solving through research, experimentation and applying the learned skills, At StemBridge Labs, we believe that learning is best when concepts can be felt, seen, grasped and implemented.

    Benefits
  • Helpful for students to enhance their interpersonal skills, increases student motivation and engagement in learning.
  • Is more effective than traditional instruction in increasing academic achievement.
  • Improves student retention of knowledge over time and Encourage students to develop a balanced, diverse approach to solve real-word problems, individually and in a team
  • Improves mastery of 21st century skills.
  • Is especially effective with lower- achieving students. -Buck Institute

Why Project Based Learning?

Because true learning comes from experimenting… The main advantage of project based learning is that it promotes problem-solving skills in real-world context. Students get engaged in problem-solving through research, experimentation and applying the learned skills

 Hands-on training Approach

Activities are customized for students based on their grade level. At StemBridgeLabs, we believe that learning is best when concepts can be felt, seen, grasped and implemented. With the successful implementation, concepts get hardwired in the mind which is the foremost objective of any education delivery model. And, PBL is the most feasible way to make it happen.
